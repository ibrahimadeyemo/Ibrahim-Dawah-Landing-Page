Du'a & Dhikr Card
Fortress of the Muslim — Essential Supplications
Morning Protection
أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ
Asbahna wa asbahal mulku lillah
"We have reached the morning and all dominion belongs to Allah." (Abu Dawud)
Protection from Harm
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ
Bismillahil-ladhi la yadurru ma'asmihi shay'un
"In the name of Allah with whose name nothing is harmed." (Abu Dawud)
Seeking Forgiveness
أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيْهِ
Astaghfirullaha wa atubu ilayh
"I seek forgiveness from Allah and repent to Him." — recite 100x daily
Sayyid al-Istighfar
اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ
Allahumma anta rabbi la ilaha illa ant...
The master of seeking forgiveness — whoever says it with certainty in the morning and dies that day enters Jannah. (Bukhari)

The 99 Most Beautiful Names of Allah
Selected Names to Reflect Upon
الرَّحْمَـٰنُ
Ar-Rahman — The Most Gracious
The One whose mercy encompasses all of creation
الْغَفُورُ
Al-Ghafoor — The All-Forgiving
The One who covers sins and forgives repeatedly, no matter how many
الصَّبُورُ
As-Saboor — The Most Patient
The One who does not rush to punish; He gives time and respite
الْوَدُودُ
Al-Wadood — The Most Loving
The One who loves His righteous servants with a love beyond measure
الْقَرِيبُ
Al-Qareeb — The Near
Closer to you than your jugular vein — always listening, always aware

🌟 Anytime Dhikr
سُبْحَانَ اللهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ
"Subhanallahi wa bihamdihi" — whoever says it 100x, their sins are forgiven even if like sea foam. (Bukhari)
Quranic Verses on the Heart
Ayat that soften, remind, and revive the soul
On Tawakkul — Trust in Allah
وَمَن يَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى اللَّهِ فَهُوَ حَسْبُهُ
Wa man yatawakkal 'alallahi fahuwa hasbuh
"Whoever relies upon Allah — then He is sufficient for him." — Qur'an 65:3
On Sabr — Patience
إِنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَ الصَّابِرِينَ
Innallaha ma'as-sabirin
"Indeed, Allah is with the patient." — Qur'an 2:153
On Tawbah — Repentance
قُلْ يَا عِبَادِيَ الَّذِينَ أَسْرَفُوا عَلَىٰ أَنفُسِهِمْ لَا تَقْنَطُوا مِن رَّحْمَةِ اللَّهِ
Qul ya 'ibadiyalladhina asrafu 'ala anfusihim la taqnatu min rahmatillah
"Say: O My servants who have transgressed against themselves — do not despair of the mercy of Allah." — Qur'an 39:53
On the Akhirah
وَمَا الْحَيَاةُ الدُّنْيَا إِلَّا مَتَاعُ الْغُرُورِ
Wa mal-hayatud-dunya illa mata'ul-ghurur
"And the life of this world is nothing but the enjoyment of delusion." — Qur'an 3:185
